Our Mission

Pet Rescuer's mission is to provide a safe home for rescued cats and dogs.

Our website currently lets you read tips on dog are as well as cat care and provides you with information to contact us.

If you have a pet to give away, you can select the "Have a pet to give away" tab and simply fill out the form. If you are looking to adopt an animal, select the "find a dog/cat" tab and fill out the appropriate form.

Our privacy statement is at the bottom of every page. By clicking on it, you can read more about how your data will be used.
